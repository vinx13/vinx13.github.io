Hi, I am Wuwei Lin. I’m a undergraduate senior student in Shanghai Jiao Tong University. I was a research assistant in Machine Vision Intelligence Group, advised by Prof. Cewu Lu. My research interests include computer vision and machine learning. I worked for Shogun this summer as a GSoC 2018 student. During that period, I have done some refactor on features, transformers and linalg, aimed at modernizing legacy code of Shogun and making it easy to use. I am now an intern in TuSimple, focused on implementing efficient int8 CUDA operators in TVM.
